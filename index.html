BLINDFALL

A JOURNEY FOR SURVIVAL

ABOUT GAME

In future England, overpopulation prompts the cultivation of triffids, Behind electric fences. A nationwide meteor shower blinds those who witnessed it, causing chaos. With the crash disrupting electricity, predatory triffids roam freely, relying on their heightened hearing to hunt humans by footsteps.
